QA System

Our Quality Assurance Protocol

At Nova SS Trading, quality sits at the heart of everything we do. Both our Fabrics and Trims & Accessories divisions follow strict quality-assurance protocols so that every product meets international benchmarks.

Our experienced in-house QA officers inspect raw materials, supervise cutting/stitching tables, and perform tests prior to any third-party inspection (SGS, Intertek, etc.).

  • Material Inspection: Thorough checking of raw yarns, chemicals, and trims.
  • Process Monitoring: Continuous supervision to maintain sizing and sewing precision.
  • Product Testing: Testing for strength, stretch, colourfastness, and eco-safety.
  • Final Inspection: Enforcing strict AQL standards on final packed cartons.
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular training for suppliers and factory audits.
