Our Specializations

Trims, Accessories & Fabrics Divisions

A comprehensive overview of our product lines, custom creations, and production capabilities.

Division 01

Trims & Accessories Division

Our Trims & Accessories wing is a trusted source for high-quality garment accents that add real retail value to finished products. We provide a single-window solution shaped around international brands and local manufacturers.

Products include: Buttons (plastic, metal, wood, shell), zippers, hook & loops, draw cords, sewing threads, size/woven/printed/care labels, leather patches, elastic bands, and complete packaging materials.

  • Elastic & Band Production (waistbands, straps, shoulder bands)
  • Decorative & Functional trims (laces, ribbons, piping)
  • Embroidery & Custom branding solutions
Division 02

Premium Fabrics Division

The Fabrics wing is dedicated to producing and supplying premium-quality fabrics for the evolving textile and fashion industries. We partner comfort with creativity, satisfying international standards and ethical sourcing.

Sourced & Supplied Fabrics: Cotton, polyester, viscose, rayon, wool, linen, silk, chiffon, georgette, satin, denim, twill, poplin, fleece, jersey knit, lycra, canvas, velvet, and organza.

  • Pocketing Fabric: TC pocketing, TR pocketing, 100% cotton sheeting, twilled pocketing, plain weave pocketing
  • Interlining: Fusible, non-fusible (sew-in), double dot fusible, powder dot, woven & non-woven interlining
  • Custom fabric finishing, dyeing, printing, coating, and functional treatments (fire retardant, anti-microbial)
