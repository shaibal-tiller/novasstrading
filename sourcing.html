Our Structured Working Process

8 stages of rigorous, transparent, and efficient buying house workflow.

01 Requirement Analysis

We start by understanding the client’s exact specifications: product type, design sheets, quantity, fabric, target pricing, and timelines.

02 Supplier & Fabric Sourcing

We engage trusted mills and production units to secure raw fabrics and trims matching specifications.

03 Sampling & Development

Creating visual and fit samples for buyer approval, tweaking measurements and colors prior to booking bulk production lines.

04 Production Monitoring

Creating daily milestone reports during sewing and dyeing to prevent bottlenecks, protecting agreed shipment seasons.

05 Quality Inspection

Rigorous inline and final inspections using AQL standards, checking stitching accuracy and fabric strength.

06 packing & Logistics

Carton packing to exact packaging briefs, shipping documents preparation, L/C handling, export custom codes verification.

07 Shipment & Delivery

Managing safe loading at Chittagong Port, freight tracking, and handling customs documents for secure port clearance.

08 After-Sales Support

Providing quick resolution, feedback collections, and client care to ensure our partnerships persist for decades.
