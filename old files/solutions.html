Architecting the Supply Chain

Discover our core operations, our B2B consultancy frameworks, and how our logistics infrastructure drives high ROI for overseas buyers.

Raw Material Sourcing & Import Infrastructure

Before a single garment is stitched, the battle for profit margin is won in sourcing. Novasstrading has established direct lines of credit with premier textile mills in China, India, and Pakistan. By bypassing middlemen, we secure premium cotton, specialized synthetics, and heavy-duty hardware (zippers/buttons) at rock-bottom prices, passing those margins directly to our Western buyers.

Third-Party Factory Consultancy (B2B Incubation)

This is where Novasstrading stands apart. We actively consult for mid-tier Bangladeshi factories. Our expert teams embed themselves into these factories to restructure their management, upgrade their compliance to EU standards, and teach them professional email and negotiation communication.

The Business Model: Once these factories meet our strict criteria, they become part of our trusted overflow network. This allows us to accept massive global orders without the risk of capacity bottlenecks.

Logistics & Financial Efficiency

Our goal is to reduce your landed cost.

Shipping & Freight

We manage all localized freight forwarding, customs clearance at Chittagong Port, and handle comprehensive L/C (Letter of Credit) documentation. We offer EXW, FOB, and CIF terms tailored to the buyer's risk appetite.

Financial Risk Mitigation

Currency fluctuations and material shortages destroy margins. We utilize forward contracts for yarn booking and maintain strict escrow protocols to ensure financial safety for both the buyer and the manufacturing unit.
