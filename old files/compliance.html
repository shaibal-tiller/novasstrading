Work Environment & Friendliness

At Novasstrading, we understand that modern RMG buyers demand absolute transparency. The days of sweatshops are over. Our factory floors are brightly lit, climate-regulated, and feature massive ventilation systems. We maintain a friendly, supportive environment with on-site medical facilities, subsidized cafeterias, and active grievance committees led by the workers themselves.

Financial Investment in Safety (CSR)

Compliance is not cheap, but it is necessary. We allocate **12% of our annual net profit** directly into Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) and infrastructural upgrades. This includes state-of-the-art automated fire suppression systems, structural load-bearing audits conducted by international engineering firms, and continuous skill-development training for our sewing operators.
