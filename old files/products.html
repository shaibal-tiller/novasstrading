Detailed Manufacturing Categories

1. High-Volume Knits & Jerseys

Our knitwear division is the backbone of Novasstrading's export volume. We specialize in 100% combed cotton, organic cotton blends, and elastane-infused activewear. Handling GSMs ranging from lightweight 120gsm to heavy 350gsm french terry.

Production Capacity: 1.2 Million Pieces / Month
Quality Standard: AQL 1.5 strictly enforced

2. Technical Woven & Denim

Moving beyond basics, our woven department handles highly technical garments requiring precision stitching. We import raw denim and twill from China and India, processing them locally through complex acid washes, stone washes, and enzyme treatments.

Production Capacity: 500,000 Pieces / Month
Focus Areas: Cargo pants, corporate chinos, heavy jackets.

Reputation, Sales Strategies & Financial Goals

How we capture market share and project future growth.

Global Sales Strategy & Client Acquisition

Novasstrading utilizes a multi-tiered B2B sales strategy. Rather than waiting for inquiries, our overseas communication hubs actively pitch to fast-fashion brands in the EU, USA, and emerging markets in South America.

We leverage Cost-and-Freight (CFR) and Free-On-Board (FOB) pricing models to give buyers maximum flexibility. By offering complete transparency in our costing sheets—from yarn price to CM (Cost of Making)—we build unbreakable trust and long-term retainer contracts with major retail chains.

Reputation & Quality Assurance (QA)

In the RMG sector, a delayed shipment or a failed audit is catastrophic. Novasstrading has built a reputation on Zero-Delay Deliveries. We implement an independent, in-house QA team that operates separately from the factory management, ensuring unbiased defect reporting before any third-party audit (like SGS or Intertek) occurs.
